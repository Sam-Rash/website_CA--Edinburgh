About Us

Welcome to the Central Asian Society of Edinburgh! We are a community dedicated to celebrating and sharing the rich and diverse cultures of Central Asia. Our society brings together students from Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan, as well as anyone interested in the region.
